Story of INFINIVERSE

In 2022, the Earth (representing the blockchain system) is overloaded, resources are exhausted. 2,500 excellent Vanguard will be selected to start the journey to discover new planets to find new energy sources.

Each of them will bring the ICP sphere representing the core values of ICP to the new land. With ICP technology, they will build the ICP ecosystem for the universe and bring resources back to fix and create a new world called THE INFINIVERSE™.

Build2Earn™ CONCEPT

Players build their #Infiniverse based on the player’s ability to combine resources, human resources, as well as strategies and personalities to earn token and upgrade the planet to a higher level.

Each game items has different power and is compatible with different resources. Players can hunt for these items by participating in solving challenges by day, week, month or trade on our marketplace.
